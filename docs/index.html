Privacy Policy

Think Like An Economist

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

Think Like An Economist does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not access your location, camera, microphone, or contacts

Local Storage

The app stores your course progress (such as which lessons you have completed) locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Think Like An Economist does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, our app is safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through an app update. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
